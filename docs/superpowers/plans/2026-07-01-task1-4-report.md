3. **f-string curly quotes**: Unicode curly quotes (`"` / `"`) inside double-quoted f-strings caused SyntaxError. Fixed by switching those preview lambdas to single-quoted f-strings.

4. **Purchasing pack**: The linter generated `_make_purchasing_pack()` and `test_purchasing.py` automatically.
